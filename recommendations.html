Brain–Computer Interfaces
Introduction Applications Critical Discussion Recommendations

Technical recommendations

  • Improve implant durability and long-term signal reliability to reduce the need for repeated procedures.
  • Implement stronger safeguards for neural data storage and transmission to protect user privacy and prevent unauthorized access.

Social recommendations

  • Provide clear, realistic education for users and the public about BCI capabilities and limits.
  • Offer psychological support for individuals adapting to implanted systems and plan for long-term device maintenance.
  • Promote fair access measures to prevent advances from widening existing inequalities.

Policy recommendations

  • Treat neural data as highly sensitive and establish strict legal protections around its collection and secondary use.
  • Require long-term safety monitoring for implanted devices and mandate transparency when companies report failures or side effects.
