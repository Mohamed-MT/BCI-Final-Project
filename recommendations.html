Brain–Computer Interfaces
Introduction Applications Critical Discussion Recommendations

Technical recommendations

  • Improve implant durability and long-term reliability to reduce repeat surgical procedures.
  • Implement stronger protections for neural data storage and transmission.

Social recommendations

  • Provide realistic public education about BCI capabilities and limitations.
  • Offer psychological and long-term support for individuals adapting to implanted devices.
  • Promote equitable access to prevent widening technological inequality.

Policy recommendations

  • Define neural data as highly sensitive information with strong legal safeguards.
  • Require long-term safety monitoring and transparent reporting for device failures or side effects.
