Actionable recommendations by stakeholder

For medical institutions (primary stakeholder)

1) Implement layered informed consent: pre-surgery, post-recovery re-consent, and ongoing consent for new data uses. 2) Add a confirmation/undo step for decoded outputs so users verify messages before they leave the device. 3) Maintain strong encrypted storage and limit retention of raw neural signals. 4) Provide long-term device support plans and psychological counseling for patients.

For device companies

Publish validation data, allow third-party audits of accuracy and bias, design fail-safe hardware, and adopt privacy-by-design (on-device processing where possible, minimal raw data exports).

For policymakers

Treat neural data as highly sensitive health data with explicit legal protections, require registries for implanted devices, mandate post-market surveillance, and fund equitable access programs.

For patients & families

Ask for plain-language explanations, request data deletion options, get second opinions before implant surgery, and plan for long-term device maintenance and psychological support.
