Brain–Computer Interfaces
Introduction Applications Critical Discussion Recommendations

Technical improvements

  • Increase implant durability and reduce surgical risks.
  • Improve signal stability and reduce scar tissue interference.
  • Strengthen security to prevent unauthorized access to neural data.

Social strategies

  • Educate the public about realistic BCI capabilities.
  • Provide psychological and training support for new users.
  • Ensure fair access to avoid widening inequality.

Policy recommendations

  • Legally classify neural data as highly sensitive.
  • Require long-term monitoring for implanted devices.
  • Mandate transparency when reporting side effects or failures.
